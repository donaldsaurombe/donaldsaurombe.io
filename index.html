The Day He Met My Dad

I met Don in 2015. Years of friendship was capped in 2018 where i realized he had boyfriend potential.I believe i had known him well enough to be worthy of meeting my parents.

December 22 I dropped a hint that my family and I will be doing our Christmas shopping. Just as i had hoped, he also decided to do his.

We were in Yayoi, a Japanese restaurant, for lunch. Donald texted me saying he was in the vicinity, i told him so was I and he should come meet my Father and sisters. I can tell he was nervous, stiff, very formal but beautifully serene on the outside nonetheless. I was only a little nervous but regardless, i silently prayed everything goes well.

My father was polite, my sisters intrigued. They KNEW he was the one right off the bat. It was quite embarrassing how they were so giddy.

On one random afternoon of December 2018 as I was begiining to do my Chrristmas shopping, we started texting and realized we were both in Megamall at that time. I was doing some window shopping and Dani, along with her dad and sisters, were having lunch at a Japanese restaurant

If my memory serves me right-before you begin judging me for being unsure just know that this moment quickly turned into a cold sweats kinda moment..- Dani asked me if I would like to meet her dad and her sisters. That caught me totally off-guard but my insticnts told me that this was a make it or break it sort of moment and I had to put aside all fears and step up to this challenge with her
